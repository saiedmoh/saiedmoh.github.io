About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering of Laval University. Prior to that, I was an Assistant Professor at the University of Quebec in Rimouski and an Affiliate Professor at Concordia University.

From 2017 to 2018 I was a Post-Doctoral Fellow in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department of Concordia University, as a member of the NSERC/Ericsson Industrial Research Chair on Model-Based Software Management.

I received my Ph.D. in computer science from the University of Montreal, in 2016, as a member of the GEODES Software Engineering Research Group. In 2010 I graduated as an Engineer in Computer Science from the National School of Computer Science (Tunisia). I also obtained my Master of Science in Software Engineering and Business Intelligence from the same school in 2011, as a member of the Triskell Team, INRIA Rennes, (France).

My research interests are at the intersection of software engineering, cloud-native systems, and software data analytics.
